WE ARE VALORANT

Defy The limit

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

PICK YOUR AGENT

4 Roles with 19 Agents that you can choose to win the match. Choose and defeat your enemies!

View All Agent

CONTROLLER

Controller agents are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.

DUELIST

Duelist agents are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.

INITIATOR

Initiator agents challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.

SENTINEL

Sentinel agents are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.
